#RPi Zero GPIO Pin Out mapping

Button | Connector Pin | Pin Desc
--- | --- | ---
1 | 11 | GPIO17
2 | 13 | GPIO27
3 | 15 | GPIO22 
4 | 29 | GPIO5 
5 | 31 | GPIO6 

#Output (WiiMote buttons)
Button | Connector Pin | Pin Desc
--- | --- | ---
A | 12 | GPIO18
B | 16 | GPIO23
C | 18 | GPIO24 
Z | 22 | GPIO25
